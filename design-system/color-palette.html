Color Psychology

Sage Green

Promotes healing and growth. Reduces anxiety while maintaining approachability. Perfect for primary actions that feel inviting rather than demanding.

Soft Blue

Builds trust and stability. Creates mental clarity and spaciousness. Ideal for informational elements that need to feel reliable.

Warm Sand

Provides comfort and grounding. Adds human warmth to clinical interactions. Used for gentle notifications and supportive messaging.
