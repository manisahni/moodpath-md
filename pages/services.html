Insurance & Payment

Cash-Pay Model

We operate on a transparent cash-pay model to provide you with the best possible care without insurance limitations. This allows us to:

  • Spend adequate time with each patient
  • Avoid insurance-mandated treatment restrictions
  • Maintain complete privacy of your records
  • Provide same-week appointments

Superbill for Reimbursement

We can provide a detailed superbill that you can submit to your insurance for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Many patients receive 40-80% reimbursement depending on their plan.

FSA/HSA Eligible

All our services are eligible for payment with Flexible Spending Account (FSA) or Health Savings Account (HSA) funds.
