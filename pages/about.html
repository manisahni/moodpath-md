Dr. Nishant Sahni, MD

A Different Approach to Mental Health

As a board-certified internist with over 15 years of experience, Dr. Sahni brings a unique perspective to mental health care. Unlike traditional psychiatry, his internist-led approach considers the whole person — examining how physical health, medications, hormones, and lifestyle factors impact mood and anxiety.

Clinical Philosophy

"Many patients with depression and anxiety have underlying medical conditions that contribute to their symptoms. As an internist, I'm trained to identify and address these root causes while providing evidence-based mental health treatment. The addition of genomic testing allows us to personalize medication selection based on your unique genetic makeup, reducing trial-and-error and improving outcomes."

Why Choose an Internist for Mental Health?

  • Comprehensive Assessment: Rule out thyroid disorders, vitamin deficiencies, and other medical causes
  • Medication Expertise: Understanding of drug interactions across all your medications
  • Whole-Person Care: Addressing sleep, nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle factors
  • Medical Monitoring: Managing side effects and monitoring metabolic health
  • Collaborative Approach: Coordinating with your other healthcare providers
