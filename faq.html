Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MoodPath MD.

General Questions

Do you accept insurance?
Currently we are cash-pay only. We provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement.
What ages do you treat?
We treat adults 18 and older.
Can you prescribe medications?
Yes, as a licensed physician I can prescribe antidepressants and related medications when appropriate.
How soon can I be seen?
Most new patients can book an appointment within a week.

Treatment Approach

What conditions do you treat?
We focus on mild to moderate depression and anxiety. We also evaluate for underlying medical causes like thyroid issues or vitamin deficiencies.
How is this different from traditional therapy?
We provide medical treatment including medication management. While therapy focuses on talk therapy, we focus on the medical aspects of mental health care.
Do you treat severe mental illness?
No. We focus on mild to moderate conditions. Severe or complex cases are referred to psychiatry.

Practical Information

Do I need a primary care provider?
Yes, we require patients to have a PCP for comprehensive care coordination. If you don't have one, we can help you find one.
Is this confidential?
Yes. All visits are confidential and HIPAA-compliant. Records are not shared without your permission.
How do online visits work?
You'll receive a secure video link before your appointment. You can join from any device with a camera and internet connection.
