Why Choose MoodPath MD?

⏰ Long Wait Times

Psychiatrist appointments often take weeks or months. With MoodPath MD, most patients are seen within days.

🏠 Accessibility

Your care is online, eliminating the stress of travel and scheduling around busy lives.

👤 Whole-Patient Care

Too often, mental health treatment ignores medical contributors like thyroid disease, vitamin deficiencies, or medication side effects. As an internist, I evaluate the whole person.

⏱️ Time Sink

Large platforms can feel impersonal and wasteful. Here, your time is respected, and each visit is efficient and focused.

🧬 Precision Tools

For patients who don't respond to first-line treatment, optional genomic testing can guide medication selection and save months of trial and error.

💰 Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees or surprise bills. Clear upfront pricing: $249 initial consultation, $119 follow-ups, with optional $89/month membership plan.

Common Questions

Do you accept insurance?

Currently we are cash-pay only, with clear upfront pricing.

What ages do you treat?

Adults 18 and older.

Can you prescribe medications?

Yes, as a licensed physician I can prescribe antidepressants and related medications if appropriate.

How soon can I be seen?

Most new patients can book an appointment within a week.

Do you treat bipolar disorder or psychosis?

No. We focus on mild to moderate depression and anxiety. Severe or complex conditions are referred to psychiatry.

How do I pay?

Secure online payment is required at booking.
