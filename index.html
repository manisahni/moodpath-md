Common Questions

Isn't depression usually treated by psychiatrists?

Mild to moderate depression is most often first evaluated and managed by internists and primary care doctors. That's the standard of care. We expand on this foundation with more time, targeted labs, and genetic testing.

Do I need a primary care provider?

Yes, we require patients to have a PCP for comprehensive care coordination and medical emergencies. We work collaboratively with your PCP to ensure you receive the best possible care.

How is this different from traditional therapy?

We provide medical treatment for depression and anxiety, including medication management and genetic testing. While therapy focuses on talk therapy, we focus on the medical aspects of mental health care.
