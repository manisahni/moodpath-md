About MoodPath MD

A modern approach to mental health care that combines medical expertise with personalized treatment plans

Why We're Different

Our internist-led approach to mental health care considers the whole person, examining how physical health, medications, and lifestyle factors impact your mood and anxiety.

Comprehensive Assessment

We rule out thyroid disorders, vitamin deficiencies, and other medical conditions that can significantly impact your mental health and mood.

Medication Expertise

Our deep understanding of drug interactions ensures safe and effective treatment across all your medications and medical conditions.

Whole-Person Care

We address sleep, nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle factors that play crucial roles in your mental health and overall well-being.

Medical Monitoring

We carefully manage side effects and monitor your metabolic health throughout treatment to ensure optimal outcomes.

Our Treatment Philosophy

We believe in evidence-based, personalized care that empowers you to take control of your mental health journey.

Evidence-Based

All treatments are grounded in the latest research and clinical guidelines, ensuring you receive care that's proven to work effectively.

Personalized

Advanced testing and individual assessment guide treatment selection, reducing trial-and-error and improving your outcomes.

Measurable

Regular assessments track your progress objectively, so you can see your improvement over time and celebrate your successes.

Collaborative

You're an active partner in your treatment decisions. We work together to find what works best for your unique needs and goals.
