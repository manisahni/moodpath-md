Track Your Mood with PHQ-9

The PHQ-9 is a validated depression screening tool used by healthcare providers worldwide. Track your symptoms weekly to monitor your progress and share results with your care team.

0-4
Minimal Depression
Normal mood, no treatment needed
5-9
Mild Depression
Watch and wait, consider CBT
10-14
Moderate Depression
Treatment recommended
15-19
Moderately Severe
Treatment strongly recommended
20-27
Severe Depression
Immediate treatment needed
