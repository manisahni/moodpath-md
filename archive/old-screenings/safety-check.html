Quick Safety Check

Takes less than 1 minute • Ensures you receive the right level of care

Our clinic specializes in straightforward SSRI/SNRI care for mild to moderate depression or anxiety. Please confirm none of the following apply today:

  • Thoughts or plans of harming yourself or others in the last month
  • Ever had mania/hypomania (very little sleep + unusually energized/risky behavior)
  • Seeing/hearing things others don't, or fixed unusual beliefs
  • Pregnant, planning pregnancy, or breastfeeding
  • Daily alcohol/drug use or substance-use treatment in the past year (including daily cannabis)
  • On MAOI/linezolid/IV methylene blue in last 14 days
  • Known long-QT/serious heart rhythm problems or on pimozide/strong QT medications
  • Advanced liver/kidney disease, uncontrolled seizures, or sodium <130 in last year
  • Currently on 2+ psychiatric medications or failed 2+ antidepressants recently
  • Under 18, or seeking benzodiazepines/stimulants
